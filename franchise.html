Franchising with Fabulous

Franchisee partners can enjoy a stable and increasing monthly income from a business that’s not only enjoyable but also highly profitable. Return on investment can be seen as soon as 18 months!! That said, this return can vary from franchise to franchise depending on your location (which city you’re in and where in that city you’re located, etc), capital investment made init ially and a few other factors. The most important factor though is your interest and motivation to become a successful business owner.

What will Fabulous do
for our franchisees

Step 1

End-to-end complete set up of your Fabulous salon

From the design to the FABULOUS in-store visuals to providing equipment that FABULOUS uses to our execution style. You’ll have a ready to serve salon!

Step 2

Train and certify your staff the Fabulous way

Every Hair Stylist and Beauty Therapist will be trained in all the services we provide. You’ll have ready-to-serve professionals.

Step 3

Product Supply

Supply all the products and services used at a FABULOUS salon.

What does a franchisee partner
need to provide?

Area

750-1500 sq ft of space (Owned or rented)

Located within city/market area on the ground or 1st floor with good frontage (at least 20-40ft)

Easy Access

Parking & Amenities

About Fabulous

At FABULOUS, we do not believe in being just GOOD; we believe in being GREAT! #Fabulous Slone

Our Co-founders, Rahul and Vaijayanti Bhalchandra, started the company in 2008 with the intention to create a beauty company that would bring the best from around the world – from practices to products to talent – to its Indian customers.

The journey began with laying the foundation for the FABULOUS Institute – a decision that showed their commitment towards education and along term plan. It was imperative that the professionals who served our clients were trained in world class techniques. Next, they sought the best ingredients and partners from across the globe to curate their products and services. Finally, they spoke to many potential customers to understand expectations and garner feedback and opened their first salon in 2023.

Now, we look forward to a new chapter with new partners to help expand the FABULOUS family all throughout India!
