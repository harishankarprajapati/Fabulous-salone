Best professional shampoo and conditioner.

Hair wash is vital for both men and women. But, how many of us use the right product according to the hair type. This question may leave you surprised. We must have good knowledge about what shampoo and conditioners we use! Fabulous Salon, one of the best salons in Bangalore offers the best shampoos and conditioners.

Things to consider before using a shampoo or conditioner! 

Scalp type matters!

The scalp type may differ from person to person. Therefore, first, know which shampoo is suitable for your scalp type and then buy one.

It’s vital to use the right shampoo because the roots of your hair are seated deep within the scalp. For example, if you have an oily scalp and dry hair, use a shampoo suitable for oily scalp and a conditioner to moisturise the dry hair.

Understand your hair type

People with dry hair should use a shampoo that is packed with additional moisture and conditioning properties. Generally, shampoos for dry hair contain extra oil and silicone. Always use sulphate and paraben-free shampoo! 
If your hair is neither dry nor oil, use a shampoo that doesn’t contain many additives. Baby shampoos are always recommended for people who are concerned about the everyday use of shampoo.
For oily hair, one should use a shampoo that removes excess oil. Normal shampoos may not suit people with oily hair as they don’t have many moisturizing ingredients.
